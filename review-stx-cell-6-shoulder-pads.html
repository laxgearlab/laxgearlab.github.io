Overview

The STX Cell 6 Gear Set is a complete three piece protection bundle including gloves, elbow pads, and a chest protector — everything you need for full upper body protection in one package. STX built the Cell 6 series to be seventeen percent thinner and thirteen percent lighter than previous models without sacrificing the NOCSAE certified protection players rely on. The result is one of the best value gear bundles on the market.

Buying all three pieces as a set saves you significant money compared to purchasing each item separately and ensures all your protection gear is matched and compatible. The D3O shock absorbing technology and GeoFlex II flexible protection throughout the set deliver performance well above the price point.

D3O Technology — What It Actually Does

D3O is one of the most advanced impact absorption materials used in sports equipment today. It flows freely during normal movement but stiffens instantly on impact to absorb and disperse the energy of a check or collision. This means the Cell 6 feels soft and flexible while you are moving but provides firm reliable protection exactly when you need it.

Combined with GeoFlex II flexible protection the Cell 6 manages to be both more protective than it looks and more mobile than traditional padded shoulder pads. This is the technology combination that sets it apart from cheaper options.

Fit & Adjustability

The premium buckle system on the Cell 6 is a genuine improvement over previous models. It locks in securely and stays put through the most physical play without loosening or shifting. The adjustable shoulder caps let you customize coverage and fit for your specific shoulder width — a feature usually reserved for more expensive equipment.

Multiple stretch zones throughout the pad allow for full range of motion on passes, shots, and checks. The removable bicep pads give you the option to customize your protection level depending on your position and playing style.

Breathability

The Cell 6 runs notably cooler than bulkier shoulder pads. The low profile design allows more airflow around the torso and the aerated construction helps regulate body temperature during intense play. Players who have switched from heavier shoulder pads consistently notice the difference in comfort during long practices and hot weather games.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the STX Cell 6 Gear Set NOCSAE certified?
Yes — all three pieces in the STX Cell 6 Gear Set meet NOCSAE standards for lacrosse protection. They are legal for all sanctioned youth, high school, and college lacrosse leagues.
How do I size the STX Cell 6 Gear Set?
STX recommends sizing based on height and weight. The Cell 6 is available in XS, S, M, L, and XL. Shoulder pads should fit snugly without restricting arm movement — the shoulder caps should sit securely over your shoulders without riding up.
Can you remove the bicep pads?
Yes — the bicep pads on the Cell 6 are fully removable. Many attack players prefer to remove them for maximum arm mobility while defenders typically keep them on for added protection.
What is D3O technology?
D3O is a rate-sensitive shock absorbing material that flows freely during normal movement but stiffens instantly on impact to absorb and disperse energy. It allows the Cell 6 to be thinner and lighter than traditional foam pads while providing equal or better protection.
