FAQs

How do I know what size lacrosse gloves to buy?
Measure from the base of your palm to the tip of your middle finger in inches. Most gloves list a hand length range per size. When in doubt, go with your dominant hand measurement and size up if you're between sizes.
How long do lacrosse gloves last?
With regular use, expect 1–2 seasons before the palm wears thin or the padding compresses. Higher-end gloves tend to last longer. Store them dry to prevent foam breakdown.
