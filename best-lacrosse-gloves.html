Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my glove size?
Measure from your palm base to middle fingertip in inches. Match to the size chart. When between sizes, go larger.
How long do lacrosse gloves last?
1-2 seasons with regular use before the palm wears thin. Store dry to extend life.
Attack vs defense gloves — what's the difference?
Attack gloves prioritize thin palms and short cuffs for feel and wrist mobility. Defense gloves prioritize thick backhand padding and extended cuffs for protection from checks.
Can I machine wash lacrosse gloves?
Hand wash in cold water with mild soap and air dry. Machine washing breaks down foam padding prematurely.
Should gloves feel stiff at first?
Yes — all gloves have a break-in period of 1-2 practices before the foam softens and molds to your hand.
