Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use football cleats for lacrosse?
Yes — football cleats are legal in most lacrosse leagues and many players use them. The main difference is weight and cleat pattern. Lacrosse-specific cleats tend to be lighter and better optimized for the cutting patterns of the sport.
Low-cut vs mid-cut — which is better for lacrosse?
Low-cut cleats offer more agility and are preferred by attackers. Mid-cut cleats give more ankle support and are popular with defenders and middies. If you have a history of ankle injuries, go mid-cut regardless of position.
Can I wear lacrosse cleats on turf?
Most lacrosse cleats work on both grass and turf, but check the cleat pattern. Aggressive molded cleats can be uncomfortable on turf. Look for "turf" or "multi-ground" versions if you play primarily on artificial surfaces.
