Our Verdict
Which Should You Buy?

Goalies: the Warrior Nemesis Pro is non-negotiable — you need a real chest protector, not shoulder pads. Field players: the STX Cell 6 shoulder pads are our top pick for protection and mobility. On a budget, the Maverik MX at $49 will get the job done for beginner and youth players.

Do all lacrosse players need a chest protector?
Goalies absolutely do — a chest protector is required and essential for safety. Field players are required to wear shoulder pads in most leagues, but these are much lighter than a full goalie chest protector. Check your league rules for specifics.
Can a field player wear a goalie chest protector?
Technically yes, but it's not recommended. Goalie chest protectors are bulky and restrict the range of motion that field players need for shooting and passing. Stick with field-specific shoulder pads for better performance.
