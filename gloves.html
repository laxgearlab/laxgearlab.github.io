Buying Guide
Sizing
Measure from your palm base to your middle fingertip. Match to the size chart. When between sizes go larger.
Palm Thickness
Thinner palms give better stick feel for attackers. Thicker palms give better protection for defenders and midfielders who take checks.
Cuff Length
Short cuffs give better wrist mobility. Standard or long cuffs give better protection. Position matters here.
Break-in Period
All gloves need 1–2 practices to break in. The foam softens and molds to your hand. Do not judge fit until after break-in.
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I find my size?
Measure palm base to middle fingertip in inches. Match to the glove size chart.
How long do lacrosse gloves last?
1–2 seasons with regular use before the palm wears thin. Store dry to extend life.
What is the difference between attack and defense gloves?
Attack gloves prioritize thin palms and short cuffs. Defense gloves prioritize thick backhand padding and longer cuffs.
Can I machine wash lacrosse gloves?
Hand wash in cold water and air dry. Machine washing breaks down foam padding prematurely.
