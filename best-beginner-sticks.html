Frequently Asked Questions

What length lacrosse stick do I need as a beginner?
Attack and midfield players use a short stick (40–42 inches). Defenders use a long pole (52–72 inches). If you're not sure what position you'll play, start with a short stick.
Do I need to restring my stick right away?
Not necessarily. All our top picks come pre-strung with decent factory pockets. As you improve, you may want to customize your pocket depth — but for your first season, the factory string job is totally fine.
What's the difference between a complete stick and a head + shaft combo?
A complete stick comes fully assembled and strung — perfect for beginners. A head + shaft combo lets you mix and match, which intermediate and advanced players prefer.
