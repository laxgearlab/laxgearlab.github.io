Frequently Asked Questions

How many sticks should my bag hold?
Most players carry 2 sticks to every practice and game — a primary and a backup. Look for a bag with at least 2 stick holders.
Do I need a ventilated pocket?
Yes — gloves and cleats get wet and smelly. A ventilated pocket keeps the smell contained and helps gear dry faster.
Backpack vs duffle — which is better?
Backpacks are better for walking long distances across campus or from parking lots. Duffles are better for car travel and tournaments where you need to pack a lot.
What size bag do I need?
A medium bag works for most players carrying a full set of gear. If you travel to tournaments frequently, consider a large duffle.
How long do lacrosse bags last?
A quality bag lasts 3-5 years with regular use. The zippers are usually the first thing to go — look for brands with reinforced zipper pulls.
