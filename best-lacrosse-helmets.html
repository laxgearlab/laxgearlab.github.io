Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace my helmet?
Cascade recommends every 5 years for competitive players, 10 years maximum. Replace immediately after any major impact or if you see cracks.
Can I buy a used lacrosse helmet?
We strongly recommend against it. You cannot verify impact history, and foam liners degrade invisibly over time. Always buy new.
What size helmet do I need?
Measure around the widest part of your head just above your eyebrows. Match that circumference to the size chart — don't guess by hat size.
Is Cascade the best brand?
Cascade dominates college and pro lacrosse. Warrior makes solid alternatives especially for youth. For most players Cascade is the safest bet.
Do helmets expire?
Yes. NOCSAE certification has a lifespan. Check the stamp inside your helmet for the manufacture date and replace accordingly.
