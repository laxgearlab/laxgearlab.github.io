Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace my lacrosse helmet?
Cascade recommends replacing helmets every 5 years for competitive players and every 10 years maximum. If your helmet takes a major impact or shows cracks in the shell or liner, replace it immediately regardless of age.
Can I buy a used lacrosse helmet?
We strongly recommend against it. You can't verify the helmet's impact history, and foam liners degrade over time even without visible damage. For a piece of gear protecting your head, always buy new.
What size lacrosse helmet do I need?
Measure around the widest part of your head (just above your eyebrows) in inches or centimeters. Most helmets list a head circumference range per size — always go by the measurement, not your hat size.
Is Cascade the best lacrosse helmet brand?
Cascade dominates the lacrosse helmet market and is worn by the vast majority of college and professional players. Warrior makes solid alternatives, especially for youth. For most players, a Cascade helmet is the safest bet.
