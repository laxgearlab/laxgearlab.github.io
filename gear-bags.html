Buying Guide
Stick Holders
Look for a bag with at least 2 external stick holders. Most players carry a primary stick and a backup to every game.
Ventilated Pocket
Gloves and cleats get wet and smelly. A separate ventilated pocket keeps the smell contained and helps gear dry faster after practice.
Backpack vs Duffle
Backpacks are better for walking across campus. Duffles are better for tournament travel when you pack everything at once.
Capacity
A medium bag works for most players. If you travel frequently or need to carry extra gear, consider a large duffle.
Frequently Asked Questions
How many sticks should my bag hold?
Most players carry 2 sticks — a primary and a backup. Look for a bag with at least 2 stick holders.
Do I need a ventilated pocket?
Yes — wet gloves and cleats cause serious odor. A ventilated pocket keeps the smell contained and helps gear dry faster.
Backpack or duffle?
Backpacks for daily use and walking to practice. Duffles for tournaments and travel when you need maximum capacity.
How long do lacrosse bags last?
A quality bag lasts 3–5 years. The zippers usually go first. Look for bags with reinforced zipper pulls.
