The Exult Rise Stick — Built For Beginners

The Exult Rise head is designed specifically for players who are still developing their catching, throwing, and ground ball skills. The wide catching area gives new players more margin for error on catches — one of the biggest struggles for beginners. The soft scoop at the bottom of the head is specifically shaped to make ground ball pickups easier, which is a skill that takes time to develop and is much harder with a narrow aggressive head designed for advanced players.

The moderate offset gives developing players natural ball control and pocket placement without the extreme offset angles used on elite attack heads. The Lock Pocket Jr. mesh system maintains consistent pocket shape all season without needing to be restrung or adjusted — huge for parents who do not know how to restring a lacrosse head and do not want to deal with a sagging or tight pocket mid-season.

4Sight+S Goggles — Certified Protection

Girls lacrosse requires goggles — they are mandatory at every level of play. The 4Sight+S Youth Goggle included in this set meets the ASTM F3077-17 safety standard for women's lacrosse and is SEI certified, meaning it is legal for all sanctioned youth, middle school, and high school leagues. You do not need to buy separate goggles — these are the real deal, not a budget substitute.

The oval wire frame design gives a wide field of vision so your daughter can see the field clearly. The silicone padding sits comfortably on the face without absorbing sweat or moisture — no wet foam pressing against her face during a hot practice. They are also easy to wipe clean after games.

STX Sidewinder Backpack

The Sidewinder backpack is a lacrosse-specific bag designed to hold the stick, goggles, and accessories a beginner player needs to get to and from practice. It is lightweight and not oversized — perfect for younger players who do not have mountains of gear yet. As she advances and acquires more equipment you would likely want to upgrade to a larger gear bag, but for year one the Sidewinder handles everything she needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age is the STX Exult Rise Starter Set designed for?
The Exult Rise is designed for beginner girls lacrosse players typically ages 6-12. It is ideal for first-year players and those in their first couple of seasons. Players who have been playing for 2 or more years and are developing serious skills will likely want to upgrade to a more advanced stick.
Are the goggles in the STX Exult Rise set league legal?
Yes. The 4Sight+S Youth Goggles included in the set are SEI certified and meet the ASTM F3077-17 safety standard required for women's lacrosse. They are legal for youth, middle school, and high school sanctioned play. Always confirm goggle requirements with your specific league before the season.
Does the stick come pre-strung?
Yes. The Exult Rise complete stick comes pre-strung with the Lock Pocket Jr. mesh system ready to play right out of the box. No restringing or pocket adjustments needed — open it and go.
What is Lock Pocket Jr.?
Lock Pocket Jr. is STX's beginner mesh system designed to maintain consistent pocket shape without requiring maintenance. Traditional lacrosse mesh can sag, tighten, or shift depending on weather and use — Lock Pocket Jr. holds its shape all season so your daughter always has the same consistent feel when she picks up her stick.
What colors does the STX Exult Rise set come in?
The STX Exult Rise Starter Set is available in Blue and Punch (pink/coral). Color availability may vary by retailer and can change seasonally — check Amazon for current in-stock options.
