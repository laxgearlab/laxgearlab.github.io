For most players, the STX Cell 6 is worth the extra spend — the hard cap and segmented foam make a noticeable difference when you take a check. Attackers who prioritize mobility over protection: the Maverik MX is your pad. Budget players can't go wrong with the Warrior Burn at $24.

Are elbow pads required in lacrosse?
Yes — arm pads are required equipment in most youth, high school, and college leagues. Check your league's rulebook for specific requirements, but in general, you need pads that cover the elbow and a portion of the forearm and upper arm.
How should lacrosse elbow pads fit?
The elbow cap should sit directly over your elbow when your arm is slightly bent. Pads that slide up or down during play are the wrong size or need tighter strapping. Measure your arm circumference at the elbow for the most accurate sizing.
