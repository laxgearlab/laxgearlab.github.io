Frequently Asked Questions

Are elbow pads required?
Yes — arm pads are required in most youth, high school, and college leagues. Check your league rulebook for specific coverage requirements.
How should they fit?
The elbow cap should sit directly over your elbow with your arm slightly bent. Pads that slide during play are the wrong size.
Hard cap vs soft foam cap?
Hard caps offer better protection against stick checks. Soft foam caps are more comfortable and flexible. Defenders should always go hard cap.
Can I wear basketball arm sleeves instead?
No — they provide zero protection and are not legal in sanctioned lacrosse leagues.
How do I keep pads from sliding?
Make sure straps are snug and consider pads with compression sleeves instead of straps — they stay in place much better during intense play.
